    📌 Attendance & Dropout Insights
    • Attendance is flat across all months — not responsive to supply delivery, suggesting structural barriers (poverty, distance) dominate.
    • Dropout reason is uniformly "Poverty and hunger" — feeding alone cannot resolve poverty-driven dropout without complementary cash transfers.
    • No seasonal pattern detected — consistent programme monitoring exists but impact on behaviour change is limited.
